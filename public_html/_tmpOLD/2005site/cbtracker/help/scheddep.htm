Entering Scheduled Deposits

Deposits can be scheduled much the same way as withdrawals.  From the "Bills" tab, you can configure new scheduled deposits such as wages, alimony and more.

Choose "New Deposit".

Enter a start date, a repeating schedule (such as weekly or monthly) and details about the transaction to be entered.

These transactions will show in the CashFlow graph, projecting how much (or little) money you will have over time
