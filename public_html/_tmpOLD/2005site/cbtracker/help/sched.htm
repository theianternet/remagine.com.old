Scheduled Transactions

CBT allows you to enter your recurring (or even one-time) transactions and will then graph a projected balance for you.

The "Bills" tab keeps track of these transactions (both withdrawals and deposits) for you.  Recurring transactions can be configured to automatically enter themselves into your checkbook register when the time comes, for instance if your mortgage is automatically deducted from your checking account every month, you can have CBT automatically enter the transaction for you automatically on that day.

The "Cash Flow" tab will then graph and list these transactions for you.
