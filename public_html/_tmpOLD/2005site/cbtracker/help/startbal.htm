Entering A Starting Balance

When you create a new file, you will be asked to enter a starting balance and a starting date.  If you will be importing data from a bank file, you may wish to put a starting balance and date that matches what the account had just before the bank file begins.  If this is a brand new account, enter the date you opened the account and your initial balance.

The initial balance will be entered as an already reconciled transaction.
