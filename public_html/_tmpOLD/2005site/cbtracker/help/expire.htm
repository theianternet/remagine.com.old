Expiring Scheduled Transactions

Sometimes you may want a scheduled transaction to repeat monthly, but expire at the end of the year.  This is good for bill payments that will end, such as a car payment or mortgage.

When configuring the scheduled transaction set "Ends at some time" to "yes" and pick a date beyond which this transaction will not recur.
