Auto-Entered Transactions

Scheduled transactions can be configured to automatically enter themselves in the register after a certain date.  On the scheduled withdrawal or deposit configuration dialog, check the "Auto-Enter this transaction" box.

The next time CheckBook Tracker is opened after the next occuring date of this transaction, it will automatically be entered into your register.  This is good for recurring payments or deposits that are always the same such as mortgage, car payment or wages.
