About Us
Salemi Appliance has been serving our valued customers in Western Massachusetts and Northern Connecticut since 1956. The store was opened by Vincent Salemi Sr. and was later passed down to his son, Vincent Salemi Jr. We Pride ourselves on Customer Satisfacton. We Sell, Service and offer extended warranties on over 30 brands of appliances. Whether you want a custom kitchen or you just need to replace a dishwasher, we can help. Give us a call, make an appointment or stop by our showroom today!
